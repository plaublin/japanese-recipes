title:  "Tamago pan"
image: tamagopan.jpg
tags: egg, bread, breakfast

ingredients:
- 2 eggs
- 2 slices of loaf bread
- mayonnaise

directions:
- boil the eggs (10 minutes)
- in a bowl, break the eggs in small pieces
- mix with mayonnaise
- add the preparation on each slice of bread
- cook in the oven for ~5 minutes
---

For 2 persons.
