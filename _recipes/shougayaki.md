title:  "Ginger pork"
image: shougayaki.jpg
tags: ginger, pork

ingredients:
- ginger
- sliced pork loin ("buta bara")
- cooking sake
- tsuyu / tare

directions:
- add cooking sake and tsuyu on the meat
- shred the ginger
- add the ginger on the meat
- in a fry pan with tsuyu, fry each slice of meat
- serve with rice
---

For 2 persons.
